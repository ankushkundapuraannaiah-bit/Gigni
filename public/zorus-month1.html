Month 1 Reference

Create a Realistic Talking AI Clone in 5 Mins

Welcome to Month 1 of Zorus 2.1. In this module, you'll learn the agentic workflow behind generating a hyper-realistic digital twin of yourself.

Module Overview

In this reference material, we explore the cutting-edge capabilities of AI-driven avatar and voice synthesis. Creating a digital twin allows for exponential scaling of content creation, personalized brand messaging, and dynamic agentic communication.

Key Learning Outcomes

  • ✓ Digital Twin Synthesis: Understand the technical requirements for mapping gestures, facial expressions, and micro-movements to a 3D avatar space.
  • ✓ Voice Cloning Integrity: Train a neural voice model that accurately mirrors pacing, intonation, and emotional resonance.
  • ✓ Agentic Integration: Implement these generative tools into a seamless, automated workflow for personal branding and client deployments.

Tech Stack & Tools

HeyGen (Avatar Gen) ElevenLabs (Voice Synthesis) Python (Automation)

Month 1 Objective

Apply the concepts from the reference video to build your own generative identity.

1
Record a 2-minute baseline video with clear lighting and audio.
2
Process the footage through HeyGen to calibrate your avatar model.
3
Clone your voice profile and generate a 30-second introductory script.
