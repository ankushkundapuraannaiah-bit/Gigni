✦ Why Gigni

Born from developer
frustrations.

Traditional hiring platforms rely heavily on resume keywords and credentials rather than direct coding capability. We built Gigni to allow student engineers and developers to demonstrate their practical execution skills transparently.

Our Mission

To democratize developer discovery. We want to enable student builders and engineers to acquire verified credentials by shipping real features, making portfolios completely transparent.

Our Vision

To establish a secure, sandbox-integrated ecosystem where recruiters run developer-submitted code directly in their browsers, bypassing initial screening filters entirely.

Our Philosophy

Write code, push builds, compile live, review output. Real builds speak louder than academic degrees or inflated job titles. Verification is our currency.

✦ How we audit

Four steps to your
verified credentials.

Earning verified status on Gigni requires hands-on execution. Developers select project briefs from the marketplace and submit functional deliverables for audit.

1

Select Brief

Pick a specialized project brief (AI clone, fullstack canvas, automation task) in the marketplace.

2

Build Features

Implement codebase code structure, write documentation, and record a walkthrough of the working build.

3

Submit to Portal

Upload your repository documents, README instructions, and video walkthrough directly from your dashboard.

4

Get Certified

Once audited, receive a cryptographically verified certificate and list your profile in the public talent list.

✦ Core Values

The core beliefs that
guide Gigni.

We prioritize hands-on engineering, fast iteration, open-source collaboration, and community metrics over gamified algorithms or bidding wars.

Authenticity Over Automation

AI tools can assist, but Gigni highlights developers who understand architecture, read code, and handle integrations manually.

Depth Over Breadth

We value developers who build deep, specialized projects with clean structures over surface-level, duplicated repositories.

AI-First Curriculums

Our educational briefs focus heavily on the modern ecosystem: LLMs, cognitive workflows, RAG systems, and AI APIs.

Community Over Competition

We foster collaboration. Freelancers showcase public builds and support peers on open source platforms.
