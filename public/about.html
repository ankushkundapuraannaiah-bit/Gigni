Our Story

We are
GIGNI.

A platform built on the belief that real skill only comes from building real things. We exist to bridge the gap between learning and doing — turning course content into verified, career-defining project experiences.

✦ Origin

Born from a frustration.

Gigni was born out of a simple observation: students were consuming more course content than ever before, yet still struggling to demonstrate real-world capability when it mattered most — in job interviews, internship applications, and real projects.

The problem wasn't knowledge. It was the absence of verified, tangible proof of building. A certificate saying "you completed this course" is fundamentally different from a certificate that says "you built a working, real-world product using these skills."

That distinction is what Gigni was created to solve. We don't just teach — we make you build. And when you build, you earn something real: a verified certificate backed by an actual working project that the world can see.

From our very first cohort out of IIT Kanpur to reaching students across India and beyond, Gigni has grown into a thriving ecosystem of builders, makers, and AI engineers who understand that experience is the new credential.

✦ How It Works

Four steps to your verified build.

Gigni's model is intentionally simple. We cut the fluff, keep the signal, and make every step count.

1

Pick a Project

Browse our library of AI-first projects — from Talking AI Clones to agentic automation pipelines and full-stack web apps powered by n8n workflows.

2

Watch & Build

Use the reference video as your guide. Then go deeper — build your own unique implementation, not just a copy. Apply your creativity and engineering thinking.

3

Upload Your Work

Submit your code, a working demo video, and a README file. Every submission is timestamped and tied to your account identity for full traceability.

4

Earn Your Certificate

After admin verification of your submission's quality and originality, you receive a verified certificate — delivered to your email and available to download from your dashboard.

✦ Core Values

The principles that shape us.

01

Authenticity Over Automation

Every submission is reviewed by a human. We don't believe in automated pass/fail systems for project evaluation. Real work deserves real review, and real certificates demand genuine verification of your build.

02

Depth Over Breadth

We'd rather you deeply master three impactful AI skills than skim through fifteen. Our curriculum is intentionally curated — every project is chosen because it teaches something that the current market genuinely demands.

03

Inclusion Without Compromise

A student from a tier-3 college deserves the same quality of opportunity as one from IIT. Gigni is designed to be the equalizer — where your work is judged on merit, not on the logo on your degree.

04

AI-First, Always

We're not retrofitting AI into an old curriculum. Gigni was built natively in the era of generative and agentic AI. Every project and skill pathway reflects the actual state of the technology landscape today.

05

Community Over Competition

Builders grow faster together. Gigni fosters a culture of collaboration — where sharing your project, getting feedback, and helping others level up is celebrated as much as completing your own submission.

06

Speed of Iteration

The AI landscape changes faster than any traditional curriculum can track. We move fast, update our projects frequently, and always ensure that what you're building today is relevant to what employers need tomorrow.
